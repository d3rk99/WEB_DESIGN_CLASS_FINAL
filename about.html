Our Story

Generations of craftsmanship and care.

We’re a family-led team shaping homes with character, warmth, and a deep respect for the people who live in them.

Family roots that guide every build.

From our first project in the late 80s to today’s modern farmhouses, we’ve always blended handcrafted details with practical layouts. Our approach is collaborative, transparent, and centered on lasting relationships.

We partner closely with clients and local trades to create homes that stand the test of time—spaces meant for loud dinners, quiet mornings, and everything in between.

People first

We believe homes should reflect the people inside them. That means attentive listening, thoughtful material selections, and honest communication from day one.

Meet the Owners

Craftsmanship guided by family leadership.

Jackson & Sam Driggs

Brothers, craftsmen, and co-owners of Iron Horse, Jackson and Sam bring complementary strengths to every project. Jackson leads design and client vision, while Sam oversees field craftsmanship and the trusted crews who bring each plan to life.

They stay hands-on from the first sketch through the final walkthrough, ensuring every detail feels intentional, welcoming, and enduring for the families who call these homes their own.

  • Direct collaboration with the owners from start to finish.
  • Design-forward layouts rooted in practical, livable craftsmanship.
  • A trusted network of artisans and trades who share their standards.

Thoughtfully crafted homes in the Northwest, made for gatherings, laughter, and quiet mornings.
